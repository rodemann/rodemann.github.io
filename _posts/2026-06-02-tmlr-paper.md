Great news: Our paper on ["Self-Supervised Laplace Approximation for Bayesian Uncertainty Quantification"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12208) has been accepted to the Transactions on Machine Learning Research (TMLR)! We have been working on this project for almost 3 years and it's great to eventually see it published in such a prestigious journal.
