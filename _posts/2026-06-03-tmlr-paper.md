Great news: Our paper on ["Self-Supervised Laplace Approximation for Bayesian Uncertainty Quantification"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12208) has been accepted to the Transactions on Machine Learning Research (TMLR)! We have been working on this project for 3 years and it's great to finally see it published in such a prestigious journal. I dedicate this work to my late supervisor Thomas Augustin, who sadly passed away shortly before the paper was accepted. It was one of the last papers Thomas had been working on. He will be missed forever.
